Responsive Breakpoints

Netinera Custom Breakpoints:

  • netinera-mobile: 360px+
  • netinera-tablet: 768px+
  • netinera-desktop: 1024px+
  • netinera-wide: 1280px+

Responsive Card Example: 

<div class="netinera-mobile:w-full max-w-netinera-card-desktop netinera-tablet:max-w-netinera-card-mobile">
  <!-- Card adapts from 368px (desktop) to 328px (tablet) to full width (mobile) -->
</div>
